WHAT IS SRM BRAIN?


The most trending thing in today’s technological world is artificial intelligence. Now most of us know what it is but how many of us actually practice it. The common notion about artificial intelligence among the learners is that it is all theory but no practical, which is to some extent correct also as they don’t have even access to the high end systems which are required to build those AI models so here is the place where SRM BRAIN steps in we will build the models on our system and you just have to use it in your code. Now this is not just useful for them who have the knowledge of AI. For people who do not have knowledge about AI but want to use it in their mobile or web application, we have created production ready APIs for all of the available pre-trained models. Be it a hackathon or making a project, SRM Brain holds your back with the heavy load of model training. So, AI is now just an API call away.
